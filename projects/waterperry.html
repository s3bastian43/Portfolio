Waterperry Gardens .

Waterperry Gardens is the home to 8 acres of beautifully landscaped ornamental gardens, a plant centre, a garden shop, a gallery, a gift shop, a museum and a tea shop. The gardens are located in Oxfordshire and are welcoming annually thousands of visitors.
As a previous Easitill customer, the company already had an eCommerce website built on an older platform but wished to do a conversion to WordPress so that the staff can manage the content in a simpler manner.

Visit Website

01. My Role

As the main developer of the project, my job was to design and build the new website, ensuring that the organisation will not lose their website identity which attracts thousands of new visitors every month.

02. Design

I started the conversion process by researching the old website so that I can get a better insight of all the elements. I wanted to ensure that the user navigation is flawless and that he can find the relevant information easily.
The colour palette was strict and an olive green and some of its shades were used on the website.

04. The Result

The old website was replaced in April 2018 and since then, it attracted over 10,000 visitors every month. The website performance and SEO were also slightly improved as the well as the staff experience in terms of managing the website.

05. Summary

As being one of the largest websites I ever worked on to that point, I felt since the moment I started, that Waterperry needs to offer a lot more than an online shop and loads of unstructured information. This is why I did my best to build a website accessible to a very large and diverse public.
