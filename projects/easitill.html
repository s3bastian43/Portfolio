Easitill.

Easitill Ltd. is a retail solutions company based in Brixworth, Northamptonshire which offers a reliable suite of systems to general retailers. During my time as a Web Designer, I also had the chance to be a part of the redesign project for our main website.

Visit Website

01. My Role

The new website was set live at the beginning of 2018 after three weeks of hard-work between the Easitill web and support teams. As a Web Designer, I had to ensure that website is structured correctly, flawless in terms of navigation, fully responsive, colourful and most importantly that it clearly represents our brand and values in the best way.

02. Design

The main idea of the website design was to represent each service offered by the company with a different colour in order to show the broad knowledge and flexibility of the Easitill staff. However, the main colours of the website had to clearly represent a brand which served customers since 1988.
My intention was, therefore, to integrate various custom coloured elements into a page but in the same way to not lose the user throughout the presentation of the services or confuse him too much.

04. The Result

After the new website was launched, we noticed that the conversion rate started to increase constantly. Now, people don’t just come to the website for a small description of the company and/or a list of the services we’re offering; they also come for support or if they want to get a better understanding of our values, an insight of the current customers and much more.

05. Summary

This project had everyone at Easitill involved in some way as each of us played a part and had a word to say regarding the final version of the website. Personally, this project helped me get a much better understanding of the company I worked for and I must say that it felt great to be a part of something this important after only a couple of months there.
